Staffing Services

We offer a consistency of commitment to quality and integrity, and are truly knowledgeable resources. Leveraging many years of market knowledge and experience, we provide top quality, cost-effective staffing solutions to our customers. We at Quiddity carefully distinguish ourselves from others agencies by remaining totally focused on Salesforce, Big Data, and SAP. We understand the intricacies of our client’s needs with respect to knowledge and technical capabilities, making sure we recognize the details of the specialized skill required. Our IT staffing solutions help you secure and optimize the most strategic and variable component to success– Customer people.
By leveraging our massive network of IT professionals and utilizing a referral-based sourcing strategy, we increase our selectivity, speed and effectiveness in finding top talent to meet Customer specific needs.
Our recruiters are trained and specialized in Salesforce, Big Data, and SAP. They are well-versed and understand our client needs and how to identify top qualify talent. The majority of our candidates are sourced via referral and recommendation. Since most of our candidates have been directly sourced by our internal recruiters or via trusted and valued sources, inherently we have qualified references to validate individuals and their skill sets.
